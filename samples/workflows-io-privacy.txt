Privacy Policy
Last Updated: July 6th, 2025

1. Introduction
Workflows Agency LLC ("Workflows.io", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our marketing automation services.

We collect personal data about you such as your name, email address, and company information when you complete forms on our website or contact us for any reason. We may also collect additional information if you voluntarily provide it during your interactions with us.

We monitor website usage including page visits, time spent on site, traffic patterns, and location data to help us understand our users and improve our services. This information may be aggregated or statistical, meaning we cannot identify you individually.

2. Information We Collect
We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

Contact information (name, email address, phone number, company name)
Project requirements and business goals
Messages and communications through our website forms
Information shared during consultations and discovery calls
We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

Website usage data (pages visited, time spent, navigation patterns)
Technical information (IP address, browser type, device information)
Analytics data to improve website performance
3. How We Use Your Information
We use your personal information for the following purposes:

To provide our marketing automation and workflow optimization services
To communicate with you about projects and respond to inquiries
To understand your business needs and deliver customized solutions
To improve our website and service offerings
To send marketing communications (with your consent)
To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
For administrative purposes and maintaining client relationships
4. Marketing Communications
We may contact you via email, phone, mail, or other methods about our services that may interest you, unless you have opted out. You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by following the instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

5. Information Sharing
We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

With service providers who help us operate our website and business (hosting, analytics, email services)
With trusted contractors who assist with project delivery
When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
In connection with a business transfer or merger
All third parties are required to keep your information confidential and use it only for specified purposes.

6. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

Secure hosting with reputable providers
Access controls limiting who can view your information
Regular security updates and monitoring
Encrypted connections for data transmission
While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices
You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
Correction: Ask us to update or correct inaccurate information
Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information
Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
Restriction: Limit how we process your information
To exercise these rights, contact us at team@workflows.io. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking
We use cookies and similar technologies to:

Remember your preferences and improve user experience
Analyze website traffic and visitor behavior
Measure marketing effectiveness
Provide relevant content

Provide our services and maintain business relationships
