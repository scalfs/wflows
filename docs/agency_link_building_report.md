## Prioritized roadmap for building links (no cold outreach)

1. **Create flagship case studies with measurable results and system names.**  
   - Study how Kinetyca names its systems (AI Outbound Engine, ABM Signal Engine, etc.) and publishes them as resource pages【207455653212003†L18-L22】【207455653212003†L32-L36】.  
   - Choose 2–3 of your core automation services (e.g., “AI Lead Gen Accelerator,” “Process Diagnostics Audit”) and develop detailed case studies that quantify results (e.g., hours saved, meetings booked).  Publish these as individual pages with descriptive URLs and infographics.  
   - Include step‑by‑step breakdowns, tool stacks and results to make them useful references.  Bloggers writing about AI automation will cite these case studies without you having to ask.

2. **Build educational frameworks and free self‑assessment tools.**  
   - Inspired by Futovia’s AI adoption cycle (Explore AI ➝ Blueprint AI ➝ Implement AI)【576681562361702†L107-L127】, create your own **“AI Automation Maturity Model”**.  Offer a short quiz or checklist to help businesses identify their stage and provide tailored tips.  
   - Develop a simple **ROI Calculator** using a spreadsheet or web app that shows potential cost/time savings from automation.  Provide it for free and allow others to embed it.  
   - Promote these resources on LinkedIn, community forums and newsletter round‑ups; they will attract backlinks from blogs and directories.

3. **Publish technical guides and thought‑leadership articles.**  
   - Write in‑depth tutorials on building specific AI agents or workflow automations.  For example, “How to build an AI prospecting agent using ChatGPT and Clay,” or “Automating CRM enrichment with GPT and Airtable.”  
   - Cross‑post these on your blog and platforms like Medium or Dev.to, and share them on LinkedIn.  Use code snippets, diagrams and free templates.  High‑quality tutorials often earn references from other blogs and newsletters.

4. **Leverage events and guest appearances.**  
   - Follow DiviUp’s example by hosting or speaking at webinars, podcasts and summits about AI operations.  Provide practical advice and share real case studies.  
   - When you speak on someone else’s platform, ensure the event page includes your bio with a link to your website【867478347661019†L28-L47】.  Offer to create a summary blog post or resource for the host’s audience, which increases the likelihood of a backlink.

5. **List your agency on relevant directories and marketplaces.**  
   - Create detailed profiles on Clutch, DesignRush, Goodish and other B2B directories, emphasising that you are a **done‑for‑you AI automation agency** and include your named systems.  Provide verified client reviews and case studies.  
   - Submit your company to smaller niche directories (e.g., AI tool aggregators, marketing agency listings).  Many listicles compile data from these directories, so having accurate, detailed profiles increases your chances of being included.

6. **Encourage client‑published case studies and testimonials.**  
   - Offer to co‑author case studies with your clients and publish them on the client’s blog or LinkedIn.  Include metrics and link back to your site.  
   - Provide clients with ready‑made testimonial templates and instructions on how to feature your work.  When the case study lives on their domain, it becomes an authoritative backlink for you.

7. **Monitor and capture unlinked mentions.**  
   - Use brand‑monitoring tools (Google Alerts, Mention) to track when your agency name appears.  For unlinked mentions in blogs or articles, send a friendly message thanking the author and suggesting a relevant page (e.g., your case study) they can link to.  Because the author already mentioned you, converting it to a link requires minimal outreach.

### Final thoughts

Becoming known as a **done‑for‑you AI automation agency** means demonstrating expertise, publishing tangible results and providing valuable resources.  By packaging your services into named systems, producing educational content and tools, and participating in industry conversations, you can attract high‑quality backlinks organically—without cold outreach.  Focus on creating assets that others want to reference; the links will follow.
