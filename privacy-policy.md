We use your personal information for the following purposes:

- To provide our automated outbound, inbound capture, RevOps, and operations automation services
- To communicate with you about projects and respond to inquiries
- To understand your business needs and deliver customized solutions
- To configure and maintain AI workflows and automation systems
- To improve our website and service offerings
- To send marketing communications (with your consent)

We may contact you via email, phone, mail, or other methods about our services that may interest you, unless you have opted out. You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by following the instructions in our emails or contacting us at contact@wflows.ai.

## 5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers:** With trusted providers who help us operate our website and business (hosting, analytics, email services, payment processors)
- **AI and automation platforms:** With third-party platforms necessary for service delivery (Clay, HubSpot, Make.com, n8n, Instantly, Retell AI, Vapi, and other tools specified in your service agreement)
- **Contractors:** With trusted contractors who assist with project delivery under strict confidentiality agreements
- **Legal requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
- **Business transfers:** In connection with a business transfer or merger

All third parties are required to keep your information confidential and use it only for specified purposes.

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Secure hosting with reputable providers
- Access controls limiting who can view your information
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Encrypted connections for data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- Secure API integrations with automation platforms
- Regular security audits of our systems

While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you

- **Deletion:** Request that we delete your personal information

- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your information
- **Portability:** Receive your data in machine-readable format (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@wflows.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

## 13. LGPD Rights (Brazilian Residents)

If you are in Brazil, you have rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), including:

- Confirmation of the existence of processing
- Access to your data
- Correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data
- Anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary or excessive data
- Portability of data to another service provider
- Deletion of data processed with your consent
- Information about public and private entities with which we share data
- Information about the possibility of denying consent and the consequences
- Revocation of consent

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at contact@wflows.ai.

## 14. GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

- Withdraw consent at any time
- Object to processing of your data
- Data portability (receive your data in machine-readable format)
- Lodge complaints with data protection authorities
- Right to be forgotten (erasure of personal data)
